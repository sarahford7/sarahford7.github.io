Lab Equipment

Incubator

We have several incubators to suit different needs, namely, aerobic microorganisms, anaerobic microorganisms and cell cultures

Spectrophotometer

The Varian Spectrophotometer enables the detection of analytes in a rapid, efficient manner. It is used for a wide range of tests, from end-point readings to kinetic readings. A water bath may also be attached to perform temperature controlled readings.

Autoclave

The lab is equipped with a regular sized autoclave that sterilizes labware and solutions that require stringent sterility conditions for experiments. The machine operates by heating water to about 120 deg celsius for about 15 minutes for effective sterilization.

Shaker

The Orbital Environ Shaker provides a temperature controlled environment for experiments that require constant mixing of samples. It is equipped temperature controls (range: 4-60 deg Celsius) and rotation speeds (up to 12600 rpm).

Eukaryotic Biological Safety Cabinet

The Biological Safety Cabinet (BSC) provides a sterile environment for experiments pertaining to cell cultures, microorganisms etc. The BSC is thorougly cleaned every month, and the filters inspected regularly to ensure proper air flow and distribution. The BSC is kept clear of objects that will disrupt the proper air flow and is cleaned with 75% ethanol before every use.

Human GI Model

This system simulates the different compartments of the human gastrointestinal (GI) tract. This in-vitro “dynamic” human GI model enables researchers to suitably investigate fate of the orally given therapeutic and other formulations. In this, model, each of the six reactors represents distinct parts of the human GI system (reactors 1-6): the stomach, the duodenum, the small intestine, the ascending colon, the transverse colon and the descending colon. This in-vitro GI model smartly mimics human GI conditions with regard to pH, bacteria, enzymes, volume, food retention time, food mixing, food therapeutic formulation and particles interactions and others.

Blood Analyzer

The blood analyzer in the lab is used for rapid and accurate analysis of analytes in the serum or other solutions. This is especially useful for large amounts of samples and numerous analytes. The machine is inspected by qualified technicians and cleaned regularly. Results are obtained by printouts from the machine.

Microencapsulator

The microencapsulator is a machine used to make micro-beads, ranging from 400um to 1mm in diameter. The different sized beads are made using different sized nozzle heads. Typically, beads are composed of alginate solution. Bead formation is based on the precise control over factors such as frequency, voltage and pressure.

HPLC

The HPLC is used to detect for small amounts of proteins or analytes in a given sample. We also have a spectrophotometer that allows for detection of analytes.

Chemical Safety Cabinet

The Chemical Safety Cabinet (CSC) provides a safe environment to perform experiments that involve toxic or hazardous chemicals. The CSC is inspected regularly and strict measures such as wearing face masks are made to ensure the safety of our members when performing such experiments.
