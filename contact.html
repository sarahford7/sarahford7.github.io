We are always looking for qualified and talented students who are interested in contributing to the areas of research we focus in.

Graduate Students
If your interests and background are strong in either cell and tissue engineering, nanomedicine, medical device engineering and/or microencapulation, you're encouraged to apply to the Graduate Program. Please send your CV and a cover letter to me directly.

Undergraduate Students
If you are an undergraduate student aiming to work on a project or summer internship, please review our research areas and projects. Email me a cover letter explaining how your skills and interests align with our lab. SURE and NSERC summer internships are available for both international and Canadian citizen/permanent resident students. You are encouraged to apply to fund your internship.
